our challenge

Our team has chosen to work on the “what is up with water” challenge, which is mainly about building an application that informs people about endangered species in the bodies of water, in addition to informing people about the water quality in these bodies of water and if they are safe for them

Go to the challenge

The main problem

Marine environments face urgent challenges: limited accessibility and uninspiring resources causing public disengagement, leading to increased pollution and endangering lesser-known species. Additionally, social media neglects the vital role of younger organisms like Foraminifera and Antarctic krill, risking the collapse of the entire ecosystem. To safeguard marine life's delicate balance, we must address these issues, raise public awareness, and prioritize the protection of foundational species in our oceans.

This innovative solution

This holistic solution revolutionizes marine ecosystem care by engaging people of all ages worldwide, with a particular focus on teenagers and youth. It offers a comprehensive platform that provides valuable knowledge about the marine environment, including water suitability for swimming, endangered species identification, and accessible facts about water origins. By bridging the information gap between generations, it promotes uniform knowledge dissemination. This initiative aims to boost global awareness and encourage active protection and nurturing of vital ecosystems, ensuring their well-being for future generations.
